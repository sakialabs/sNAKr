## Mission

Build open, local-first inventory intelligence that helps households:

- stay stocked without perfect tracking  
- reduce waste through early, explainable signals  
- coordinate shared living with zero blame  
- restock smarter by connecting to Nimbly and the wider ecosystem

**a steady home.**

## Manifesto

### 1) We keep it human
Fuzzy states beat fake precision.  
If people need a spreadsheet, we failed.

### 2) We do not shame
No guilt. No nagging. No drama.  
Life is already heavy.

### 3) Households are a commons
A shared kitchen is a shared system.  
We build for cooperation, not policing.

### 4) We prevent waste on purpose
Waste is the enemy: money, food, time, attention.  
sNAKr exists to stop the quiet bleed.

### 5) We stay explainable
Predictions must be understandable.  
Confidence must be visible.  
Automation must be earned.

### 6) We are local-first by design
Resilience means nearby options, practical swaps, and steadier habits.  
Not ‚Äúcheapest at any cost.‚Äù

### 7) We are open-source on purpose
This is grassroots infrastructure.  
Auditable. Forkable. Adaptable.  
A tool people can shape, not a black box that shapes people.

### 8) We build the bridge to action
sNAKr is awareness.  
Nimbly is the move.  
Together: fewer surprises, better timing, more breathing room.
